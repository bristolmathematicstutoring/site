Experience & Approach

I have 5 years of experience teaching mathematics at university and doing mathematical research and will use my mathematics background to tailor material to help you grow and develop. I've run multiple undergraduate tutorial groups, drop-in sessions, supported problem-classes, marked and provided feedback for homeworks and assessments at UoB. Topics span: Linear Algebra, Analysis, Number Theory, Functional Analysis, Group Theory, ODEs, (Multivariable) Calculus, Projects for Data Science, Mathematics for Computer Science, Algorithms in Computer Science, Coding in C, R & Python.

We can go through problem-solving, school curriculum (GCSE, A-LEVEL), university entrance, prep, university help or mathematics beyond for professional work. I can facilitate extracurricular projects for interested students. Tutoring will be tailored to your interests and goals. My approach in teaching is student lead. Sessions will be relaxed and non-judgemental and will focus on being present engaging with the necessary mathematical content to help you progress. We'll engage with concepts, particular examples and problem solve. There are no shortcuts. I look forward to starting this journey with you!
